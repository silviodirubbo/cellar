Geneva

Friendly wine tastings
in Geneva.

A structured yet convivial way to share moments together while discovering the complexity of the wine world — one bottle at a time.

This website is the infrastructure behind those evenings. A place to keep track of what we have tasted, to plan what comes next, and to make it easier for friends to join, propose, and shape where we go. Not a single drop wasted.

Upcoming tastings also show an indicative cost — this refers only to the expected price of the bottles used for that evening. Nothing is charged on top. Wine prices can vary sensibly depending on the theme, so you can choose when to join based on what you feel like spending that month.
